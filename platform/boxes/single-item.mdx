---
title: "Single item box"
description: "Subscriptions that ship one item per cycle"
---

A single item box ships one product per cycle. Each subscription is tied to a single product variant, and the customer receives that one item on the cadence you set.

## When to use it

Use a single item box when the customer's subscription is a one-to-one mapping to a product. For example:

- One pair of socks every month
- One bag of coffee every two months
- One bottle of supplements every three months

## How it works

Each subscribable product has its own selling plan. When a customer subscribes, the cart contains one line: the selected variant with its selling plan attached. Every recurring cycle generates an order with that single line.

Single item boxes work with both **fixed** and **dynamic** experiences:

- **Fixed**: The same variant ships every cycle.
- **Dynamic**: The assignment engine selects a variant from the eligible pool each cycle, scoped by the customer's theme and preferences.
