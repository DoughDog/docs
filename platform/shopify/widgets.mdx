Dough Dog ships a set of Shopify storefront widgets that render on the product form. They give customers the controls they need to subscribe and to manage their existing subscription from a product page.

## Subscription purchase options

Renders on the product form as a selling plan selector. The customer chooses between a one-time purchase and any of the available subscription frequencies for the product. Selecting a subscription option attaches the corresponding `selling_plan` to the line that gets added to the cart.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/widgets/purchase-options.png" alt="Subscription purchase options widget showing a one-time purchase option and a discounted monthly subscription option" />
</Frame>

The options shown are driven by the selling plans Dough Dog has attached to the product. See [Selling Plans](/platform/shopify/selling-plans) for how those get created and synced.

## Subscription add to order

Renders a button on the product page that lets an active subscriber add the product to their next upcoming shipment. If a subscriber discount is configured on the plan, the displayed price reflects that discount, and the line is added at the discounted price.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/widgets/add-to-order.png" alt="Subscription add to order button showing 'Add To May 19 Subscription Order - $28.80'" />
</Frame>

This widget also lives on the product form. It only appears for customers who have an upcoming subscription order eligible to receive add-ons.
